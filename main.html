Summary

Inception is a mind-bending thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. It follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who enters people’s dreams to steal secrets. Given a chance to erase his criminal record, he must plant an idea deep within a target's subconscious, but his past haunts him at every step.
